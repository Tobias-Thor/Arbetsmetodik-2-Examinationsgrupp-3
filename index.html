Welcome to Taverna – A New Era of Flavor and Community! With over 40 years of culinary excellence, Taverna is stepping into the future with a new concept and renewed passion. From the classic tastes of Italian cuisine to a bold transformation – we are proud to introduce our new focus: American BBQ at its finest. Under the leadership of new owners, Taverna has been revitalized, where tradition meets innovation. Here, you’re invited into a world of sizzling grills, juicy cuts of meat, and rich flavors that evoke the heart of the American South. Every dish is crafted with care and fire, using only the finest ingredients to create an authentic BBQ experience. Our ambiance is as tasteful as our food – a harmonious blend of modern design and rustic charm. You’ll find a relaxed atmosphere that makes every visit memorable, whether it’s for a family dinner, an after-work gathering, or a festive evening with friends.

Grand reopening in early 2025 – An experience worth the wait.

Stay tuned and get ready to let your taste buds dance in an atmosphere that celebrates both history and innovation. Taverna is ready to become your new favorite destination for food and togetherness. We can’t wait to welcome you!
